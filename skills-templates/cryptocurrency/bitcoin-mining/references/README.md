# Bitcoin Mining Documentation - Reference Materials

This skill provides comprehensive Bitcoin mining knowledge compiled from four authoritative industry PDF documents.

## Source Documents

### 1. Bitcoin Mining 2024 Year in Review
**File**: `docs/bitcoin.mining.2024.pdf`

**Topics Covered**:
- 2024 Bitcoin mining industry landscape
- Global hashrate distribution and trends
- Network difficulty adjustments and metrics

- Industry challenges and opportunities

**Target Audience**: Industry analysts, mining operators, investors

### 2. Bitcoin Mining Economics
**File**: `docs/bitcoin.mining.economics.pdf`

**Topics Covered**:
- Mining profitability calculations and models

**Target Audience**: Financial analysts, mining business operators, investors

### 3. Bitcoin Mining Handbook
**File**: `docs/bitcoin.mining.handbook.pdf`

**Topics Covered**:

- Mining infrastructure setup and deployment

- Monitoring systems and alerting
- Maintenance procedures and schedules
- Troubleshooting common issues
- Best practices for operations

**Target Audience**: Mining operators, technical staff, facility managers

### 4. Bitcoin Mining Heat Reuse
**File**: `docs/bitcoin.mining.heat.resue.pdf`

**Topics Covered**:
- Waste heat recovery technologies
- Thermal system design for mining facilities
- Heat reuse applications (heating, greenhouse, industrial)

- Sustainable mining practices
- Renewable energy integration
- Environmental impact mitigation

**Target Audience**: Sustainability officers, facility designers, green energy advocates
